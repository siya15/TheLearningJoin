What are learning types

Visual learners
These learners understand material most efficiently through sight.
Some examples of study resources for these learners are:
• Images
• Diagram
• Drawings
These types of learners can easily remember people’s faces, places, and shapes.

Auditory
Also known as aural learners, these types of learners process concepts most efficiently through hearing.
Some examples of study resources for these learners are:
• Listening to lectures
• Listening to audio recordings
• Talking aloud to themselves
These people can easily remember the words that they heard.

Readers & Writers
Reading and writing learners understand concepts most efficiently through reading and writing.
Some examples of study resources for these learners are:
• Books
• Essays
• Worksheets These learners can easily remember what they read or wrote.

Kinesthetic
Also known as tactical learners, kinesthetic learners process material most efficiently through touch.
Some examples of study resources for these learners are:
• Experiments
• Testing
• Physical activities
Kinesthetic means “the sensory perception of movement.”
